At TBBLUSA.ORG, we recognize that the urban environment is the most critical area for our defense. We do not force the issue; we provide the solution. Young Politicians™ is our flagship initiative designed for the kids of Philadelphia—taking those who have lived through the struggle and teaching them the Mechanics of the Republic.

Legislative Literacy

Training youth to analyze governmental laws and understand the civilian leadership necessary to help the community. We teach them to draft the very ordinances that protect our blocks.

The Dual Lens

Teaching kids that adhering to governmental laws is the civic mirror of adhering to spiritual truth. We show them that the best leaders are those who have seen through the furnace and learned from it.

Urban Defense

Equipping our youth to defend our vision. They are the lifeline of our city, our state, and our world. This program turns potential recidivism into permanent professional leadership.
