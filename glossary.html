Vellum v1 Module

The core brain. A titanium-encased NPU designed for local, private AI processing without cloud reliance.

24-Pin Gold Array

The skeletal bridge. Located at the base, these pins allow the brain to dock into the physical robotic frame (Phase 2).

Witness Sensors

A dual-array of 12MP cameras and quad-mics that allow the device to "Witness" and protect its user in real-time.

Inherent Data

The philosophical principle that a user's data is part of their "Digital Soul" and cannot be sold or harvested.
