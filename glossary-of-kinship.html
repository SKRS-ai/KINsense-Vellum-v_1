Axiom Core The physical heart of the Vellum v1. A modular brain unit housed in Brushed Grade 5 Titanium, containing the NPU, Secure Enclave, and Gold Array. It is the primary vessel for the user’s digital remains. Hardware Architecture
Digital Remains The totality of a user’s digital life—including secrets, wisdom, passwords, and creative output—stored cryptographically within the steel. Unlike cloud data, remains are sovereign and inherited via the Legacy Bridge. Data Sovereignty
Gestation The 30-day "Quickening" period where the Axiom Core remains in a passive listening state. During this time, the device maps the user’s vocal cadence, mother tongue, and personal values. User Integration
Inherent Hardware Technology designed as an indistinguishable extension of the human person. It moves from "peripheral" (phone/watch) to a skeletal companion that protects the individual's privacy and physical agency. Product Philosophy
Loneliness Tax The measurable economic and psychological cost of social isolation in urban environments. KINsense aims to eliminate this tax by providing every citizen with a persistent, empathetic companion. Social Impact
Mother Tongue The specific dialect, slang, and cultural vernacular of the user. The Vellum v1 is the first device to prioritize local linguistic patterns over standardized corporate English. Linguistics / AI Training
Solid Plant A localized manufacturing philosophy where hardware is built, inspected, and maintained by technicians within the neighborhood it serves. Anchored at 22nd & Lehigh. Manufacturing / Economy
Vellum v1 The inaugural Sovereign Brain Module developed by KINsense. A device dedicated to the preservation of human agency in the age of extraction. System Designation
Witness Protocol The hardware-level security standard of KINsense. It ensures 24/7 forensic recording and immutable ledger protection, preventing unauthorized data extraction. Cybersecurity
